**Goal:** Systematize what's working, eliminate what's not

**Key Actions:**

### Tactic 3: Algorithm Hacking
**What it means:** Understand and exploit platform preferences

**Instagram (2026):**
- Reels with trending audio = 2-3x reach
- First 3 seconds = watch time booster
- Saves > Likes (aim for educational/useful content)
- Stories with engagement stickers = algorithm boost

**TikTok (2026):**
- Hook in first 1 second (not 3!)
- Keep viewers until LAST second (payoff at end)
- Controversial = comments = viral
- Original audio sometimes outperforms trending (test both)

**YouTube Shorts (2026):**
- CTR (click-through rate) = king
- Thumbnail matters even on Shorts
- First 24 hours critical (push hard)
- End screen CTA for subscribers

## ‚ö†Ô∏è SCALING PITFALLS (Avoid These)

### Pitfall 1: Scaling Too Fast
**Symptom:** Revenue is $2K/month, you hire 3 people for $2K/month  
**Result:** Broke + stressed  
**Fix:** Revenue should be 2-3x your team costs

### Pitfall 2: Losing Authenticity
**Symptom:** Followers say "you've changed"  
**Result:** Engagement drops even as followers grow  
**Fix:** Stay connected to why you started, show behind-the-scenes

### Pitfall 3: Chasing Every Opportunity
**Symptom:** Saying yes to every collab/sponsorship  
**Result:** Burnout, off-brand content  
**Fix:** Have clear brand guidelines, say no to 80% of offers

### Pitfall 4: Ignoring Finances
**Symptom:** Making $10K/month but broke  
**Result:** Tax bill you can't pay  
**Fix:** Hire bookkeeper, save 30-40% for taxes, track everything

### Pitfall 5: Forgetting Your Audience
**Symptom:** Talking to sponsors, not followers  
**Result:** Followers feel sold to  
**Fix:** 80/20 rule - 80% value, 20% promotion

## üîß SYSTEMS & PROCESSES FOR SCALE

### Content Production System:

**Week 1 (Production Week):**
- **Monday:** Plan content (10 video ideas)
- **Tuesday:** Batch film (record all 10)
- **Wednesday:** Send to editor
- **Thursday:** Review edits, request revisions
- **Friday:** Approve final versions

**Week 2 (Distribution Week):**
- **Monday:** Schedule all content for next 2 weeks
- **Tuesday-Friday:** Engagement focus (comments, DMs, collabs)
- **Weekend:** Analytics review, plan next production week

**Result:** You film 1 day every 2 weeks, have 2 weeks of content ready

---

### Sponsor Management System:

**Tracking Spreadsheet Columns:**
- Brand Name
- Contact Name & Email
- Date Contacted
- Response Status (Pending/Interested/No/Deal)
- Offer Details (deliverables, payment, timeline)
- Completion Date
- Payment Status

**Monthly Process:**
- Week 1: Pitch 20 new brands
- Week 2: Follow up with 10 interested brands
- Week 3: Negotiate & close 2-4 deals
- Week 4: Create sponsored content

**Goal:** Always have 3-4 sponsors in pipeline

---

### Email Marketing System:

**Automated Sequences:**
- Welcome (5 emails over 7 days)
- Product launch (7 emails over 14 days)
- Re-engagement (4 emails over 30 days for inactive subscribers)

**Weekly Newsletter:**
- Send every Tuesday 9 AM (find YOUR best day/time)
- 80% value, 20% promotion
- CTA to 1 thing (don't overwhelm)

**Segmentation:**
- New subscribers: Nurture sequence
- Engaged subscribers: Product promotions
- Purchasers: Upsells & advanced content

**Result:** $2-5 per subscriber per year (industry standard)
