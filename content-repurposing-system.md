## Overview & Philosophy

**Core Principle:** Create once, distribute everywhere, maximize ROI.

**Time Investment:**
- Initial video: 2-4 hours
- Repurposing: 2-3 hours
- **Total content created:** 20+ pieces
- **Effective time per piece:** ~10-15 minutes

**The Multiplier Effect:**
One 30-minute video â†’ 20+ content pieces â†’ 60+ days of consistent posting across 6 platforms.

## Phase 2: Platform-Specific Optimization

### YouTube (Main Platform)

**Full Video:**
- **Specs:** 1920x1080, 16:9, H.264, 30fps
- **Length:** 8-60 minutes (aim for 8-15 for retention)
- **Thumbnail:** 1280x720, text overlay, high contrast
- **Title:** 60 chars, keyword front-loaded
- **Description:** 5,000 chars, timestamps, links, keywords
- **Tags:** 15-20 relevant tags
- **Best posting time:** 2-4 PM EST, Thu-Sat

**YouTube Shorts:**
- **Specs:** 1080x1920, 9:16, under 60s
- **Hook:** First 1-2 seconds critical
- **Captions:** Hardcoded, large text
- **CTA:** Subscribe prompt in video
- **Frequency:** 1-2 per day
- **Best time:** 6-9 AM, 12-3 PM, 7-10 PM

### Instagram

**Reels:**
- **Specs:** 1080x1920, 9:16, 30-90s (aim for 7-15s)
- **Aspect ratio:** MUST be 9:16 (full screen)
- **Cover image:** Set custom thumbnail
- **Audio:** Trending sounds boost reach
- **Captions:** 2,200 chars, front-load value
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 highly relevant (not 30)
- **Best time:** 11 AM, 1 PM, 7-9 PM local
- **Frequency:** 3-5 reels/week

**Carousel Posts:**
- **Specs:** 1080x1080, 1:1, up to 10 slides
- **Design:** Consistent branding, easy to read
- **Hook:** Slide 1 must grab attention
- **Flow:** Tell a story across slides
- **Last slide:** CTA (save, share, follow)
- **Best time:** 10 AM, 2 PM Wed-Fri

**Quote Cards:**
- **Specs:** 1080x1080, 1:1
- **Font:** Large, readable (60pt+)
- **Colors:** Brand colors, high contrast
- **Logo:** Small watermark

### TikTok

**Video Specs:**
- **Format:** 1080x1920, 9:16, 15-60s
- **Optimal length:** 7-17 seconds (highest engagement)
- **Hook:** 0.5-1 second or you're scrolled past
- **Captions:** Auto-captions ON + manual check
- **Sounds:** Use trending audio (check TikTok Creative Center)
- **Text overlay:** Large, easy to read on mobile

**Algorithm Hacks:**
- Post 1-3x per day
- Best times: 6-10 AM, 7-11 PM EST
- Reply to comments with video responses
- Duet/stitch trending content
- Watch time > likes (keep them watching)

**Hashtags:**
- 3-5 hashtags max
- Mix: 1 trending + 2 niche + 1-2 branded
- Avoid spam tags

### LinkedIn

**Video Posts:**
- **Specs:** 1920x1080 (16:9) or 1080x1080 (1:1)
- **Length:** 30-90 seconds (native video performs better)
- **Captions:** Required (80% watch muted)
- **Style:** Professional but authentic
- **Hook:** "Here's what I learned..."
- **Best time:** Tue-Thu, 8-10 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM

**Carousel Posts (PDF):**
- **Specs:** 1080x1080, saved as PDF
- **Slides:** 8-12 slides
- **Design:** Professional, data-driven
- **Template:** Canva "LinkedIn Carousel"
- **Content:** Tips, frameworks, case studies
- **High engagement:** People comment "Commenting for reach"

**Text Posts:**
- **Length:** 150-300 words (sweet spot)
- **Hook:** First 2 lines visible in feed
- **Format:** Short paragraphs, line breaks
- **Style:** Story-based, personal lessons
- **Frequency:** 3-5x per week

### Twitter/X

**Video:**
- **Specs:** 1920x1080 or 1:1, under 2:20 (free accounts)
- **Length:** 30-60 seconds optimal
- **Captions:** Hardcoded
- **Preview:** First frame should hook
- **Best time:** 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM EST

**Text Threads:**
- **Hook tweet:** Must stop the scroll
- **Thread length:** 5-10 tweets
- **Format:** One idea per tweet
- **Engagement:** Ask question in last tweet
- **Tip:** Number tweets (1/10, 2/10...)
- **Best time:** 8-9 AM, 5-6 PM EST

**Quote Tweets:**
- Pull 10-15 quotes from video
- Add 1-2 sentence context
- Post 1-2 per day from one video

### Pinterest

**Idea Pins (Video):**
- **Specs:** 1080x1920, 9:16, up to 60s
- **Slides:** 2-20 slides (can mix video + static)
- **Title:** 100 chars, keyword-rich
- **Cover:** Eye-catching, text overlay
- **Best for:** Tutorials, how-tos, tips

**Static Pins:**
- **Specs:** 1000x1500 (2:3 ratio) - CRITICAL
- **Title:** 100 chars, SEO keywords
- **Description:** 500 chars, keyword-rich
- **Alt text:** Describe image for accessibility
- **Board:** Relevant, active board
- **Best time:** 8-11 PM EST (planning for tomorrow)

**Pin Types from Video:**
1. Quote pins (5-7)
2. Tip compilation pins (3)
3. Infographic-style pins (2-3)
4. Video tutorial pins (2-3)

3. Create vertical crop template (1080x1920)

- Format: MP4

   - Export all as PNG

   - Template: "Instagram Carousel"
   - 10 slides with key takeaways
   - Consistent fonts/colors
   - Export as individual PNG

3. **LinkedIn Carousel (45 min):**
   - Use "LinkedIn Carousel" template
   - Professional style
   - Export as PDF

**Canva Batch Tips:**
- Create templates with your brand colors
- Duplicate slides, change text
- Use "Magic Resize" (free for some formats)
- Save frequently used elements

**Wednesday: Pinterest + Thumbnails (1.5 hours)**

1. **Pinterest Pins (1 hour):**
   - 2:3 ratio (1000x1500)
   - Text overlay with value prop
   - Keyword-rich titles
   - Create 5-7 pins

2. **YouTube Thumbnail (30 min):**
   - 1280x720
   - High contrast colors
   - Large text (70pt+)
   - Your face (if applicable)
   - Test on mobile view

**Thursday: Text Content (2 hours)**

1. **Blog Post (1 hour):**
   - Use transcript as base
   - Expand with research
   - Add headings (H2, H3)
   - Insert video embed
   - SEO optimize (Yoast free)

2. **Twitter Thread (30 min):**
   - Extract 10 best quotes
   - Write 7-10 tweet thread
   - Add context to each
   - Format in notes app first

3. **LinkedIn Posts (30 min):**
   - Write 3 standalone posts
   - Personal stories from video
   - 150-300 words each

**Friday: Finalize & QA (1 hour)**
- [ ] Review all content
- [ ] Check captions/typos
- [ ] Ensure brand consistency
- [ ] Organize in folders
- [ ] Fill in content calendar

## Advanced Tips & Hacks

### Hook Formula for Short-Form

**Proven hook patterns:**
1. "Here's what I learned spending $X on Y"
2. "Do this, not that" (direct contrast)
3. "X things nobody tells you about Y"
4. "I tried X for 30 days, here's what happened"
5. "Stop doing X. Do this instead."
6. Pattern interrupt (unexpected statement)

**First frame:**
- Your face (if personality brand)
- Bold text
- Intriguing visual
- Movement

**First 1 second = 50% of your success.**

### Thumbnail Mastery

**Winning elements:**
- High contrast colors (yellow, red, blue)
- Large text (readable on mobile)
- Your face with expression
- Only 3-5 words
- No clutter

**A/B test:**
- Upload multiple versions
- Compare CTR
- Keep winner

**Tools:**
- Canva (design)
- TubeBuddy (A/B testing, free tier)

### Caption Strategies

**Instagram:**
- Hook in first line (before "...more")
- Line breaks for readability
- CTA in middle (not end)
- 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Tag relevant accounts

**TikTok:**
- Open loop (don't give away ending)
- Ask question
- Encourage duets/stitches
- Keep it short (100 chars)

**LinkedIn:**
- Personal story first
- Professional insight
- Formatting: lots of line breaks
- CTA: "What's worked for you?"

**Twitter:**
- Thread format for longer content
- One idea per tweet
- Number your tweets (1/7, 2/7...)
- Visual breaks (emojis, line breaks)

### Repurposing Hacks

**Horizontal to Vertical:**
- Use CapCut "Auto Reframe" (tracks subject)
- Or: Zoom in + crop
- Or: Add background blur of same video
- Or: Film twice (horizontal + vertical)

**Add value without refilming:**
- Text overlays with extra tips
- B-roll on top
- Screenshots
- Animations
- Memes

**Extend video lifespan:**
- Repost top performers after 3-6 months
- Update thumbnail/title

- Fresh perspective in caption

**Result:** 2-3 months of content in 1 day.

### Content Recycling System

## Troubleshooting & FAQ

### "I don't have time for all this!"

**Start small:**
- Week 1: Just YouTube + TikTok (2 platforms)
- Week 2: Add Instagram
- Week 3: Add LinkedIn or Twitter

**Or:** Use "Lazy Genius" workflow (3-4 hours/week for 10 pieces).

### "My shorts aren't getting views"

**Common issues:**
- âŒ Weak hook (first 1 second)
- âŒ No captions
- âŒ Wrong aspect ratio (not 9:16)
- âŒ Posted at bad time

**Fix:**
- Test 3 different hooks on same clip
- Always use captions
- Check export settings
- Post 7-10 PM local time

### "I'm overwhelmed by all the platforms"

**Cut ruthlessly:**
- Pick 2-3 platforms where your audience is
- Go deep instead of wide
- You don't need to be everywhere

**Best combos:**
- B2B: YouTube + LinkedIn + Twitter
- B2C: TikTok + Instagram + Pinterest
- Education: YouTube + Twitter + Blog

### "How do I know what content to repurpose?"

**Criteria for good repurposing candidates:**
- âœ… Evergreen content (not time-sensitive)
- âœ… Clear takeaways/lessons
- âœ… Visual or story-based
- âœ… Modular (sections can stand alone)

**Not great:**
- âŒ News/current events
- âŒ Heavily edited/produced (hard to extract)
- âŒ Long monologues (no variety)

### "My captions are wrong"

**Quick fixes:**
- Use CapCut auto-captions (better than YouTube)
- Edit in the app (click words)
- Keep jargon/brand names in custom dictionary
- Budget $5-10 for Rev.com if critical

### "Should I repurpose old content?"

**Yes!** Your back catalog is a goldmine.

**Process:**

3. Update facts/info if needed
4. Repurpose using this system
5. New audience hasn't seen it
