## B-Roll Shot Lists

### Category 1: **Establishing Shots**
*Purpose: Set the scene, show location*

1. **Wide cityscape/landscape** - 5-8 seconds
2. **Landmark from distance** - 4-6 seconds
3. **Street-level pan** - 6-10 seconds (slow)
4. **Aerial/high angle** - 5-8 seconds
5. **Time-lapse of location** - 3-5 seconds

**Camera Settings:**
- 24fps for cinematic, 60fps for slow-mo options
- Wide angle lens
- Stable (tripod/gimbal)

---

### Category 2: **Detail Shots**
*Purpose: Add texture, visual interest*

1. **Food close-up** - Multiple angles
2. **Hands interacting** - Pouring coffee, opening door
3. **Texture close-up** - Fabric, walls, nature
4. **Product/object** - Rotating or static
5. **Signage/text** - Readable, 3-4 seconds
6. **Eyes/face detail** - Focus pull
7. **Feet walking** - Ground-level
8. **Transportation details** - Wheels, windows

**Camera Settings:**
- 60fps for smooth motion
- Macro or portrait mode
- Shallow depth of field

---

### Category 3: **Action/Motion**
*Purpose: Energy, engagement*

1. **Walking towards camera** - Confident stride
2. **Walking away from camera** - Exploration vibe
3. **Jump/motion** - 60fps minimum, 120fps ideal
4. **Driving POV** - Dashboard or windshield
5. **Running/activity** - Sports, playing
6. **Hair flip/movement** - 120fps slow-mo
7. **Throwing object** - Catching motion
8. **Spinning/twirling** - Full rotation

**Camera Settings:**
- 60-120fps for slow-mo
- High shutter speed (1/120+)
- Follow/track subject

---

### Category 4: **Transition Helpers**
*Purpose: Smooth scene changes*

1. **Camera whip pan** - Fast horizontal movement
2. **Subject crossing frame** - Complete pass
3. **Door opening/closing** - POV
4. **Obstruction wipe** - Object passes lens
5. **Light to dark** - Entering building
6. **Dark to light** - Exiting tunnel/door
7. **Reflection wipe** - Window, mirror
8. **Spin/rotation** - 360¬∞ or partial

**Camera Settings:**
- 24-30fps for natural blur
- Handheld for organic feel
- Overlap at least 1 second with adjacent clips

---

### Category 5: **Atmospheric**
*Purpose: Mood, emotion*

1. **Golden hour backlit** - Silhouettes
2. **Rain/water droplets** - Window or ground
3. **Fog/mist** - Landscapes
4. **Fire/candles** - Close-up flicker
5. **Plants/leaves** - Gentle movement
6. **Clouds moving** - Time-lapse
7. **Water movement** - Ocean, river, fountain
8. **Bokeh lights** - Out of focus background

**Camera Settings:**
- Wide aperture for bokeh
- 24fps for natural motion
- Stabilized for smooth atmospheric shots

---

### Category 6: **People/Lifestyle**
*Purpose: Human connection*

1. **Candid laughter** - Genuine moments
2. **Looking off-camera** - Contemplative
3. **Interaction with locals** - Handshakes, talking
4. **Group shot** - Friends, family
5. **Solo portrait** - Multiple angles
6. **Activity engagement** - Cooking, working
7. **Reaction shots** - Surprise, joy

**Camera Settings:**
- Portrait mode or f/1.8-2.8
- Eye tracking autofocus
- Natural lighting preferred

---

### Category 7: **Travel-Specific**
*Purpose: Journey narrative*

1. **Packing/unpacking** - Time-lapse
2. **Passport/tickets** - Close-up
3. **Map/planning** - Pointing, tracing
4. **Airport/station** - Crowds, departures board
5. **Transportation interior** - Plane window, train seat
6. **Checking in** - Hotel, accommodation
7. **Currency/payment** - Local money
8. **Souvenirs** - Shopping, holding items

---

### **Shot Ratio Guide**
For a 60-second video:
- 30% Establishing/wide shots
- 25% Detail shots
- 20% Action/motion
- 15% Transitions
- 10% Atmospheric/people

**Pro Tips:**
- Always shoot 3-5x more B-roll than you think you need
- Get the same shot from 3 different angles
- Hold each shot for 5+ seconds (even if you only use 2s)
- Shoot in sequences: wide ‚Üí medium ‚Üí close-up
- Capture ambient sound for each location

## Thumbnail Design Formulas

### Formula 1: **Face + Text + Contrast**
**CTR Benchmark:** 8-12%

**Elements:**
- **Face:** Close-up, exaggerated expression (surprise, excitement)
- **Position:** Left or right third, looking toward center
- **Text:** 3-5 words maximum, bold sans-serif
- **Size:** Text takes up 30-40% of thumbnail
- **Colors:** High contrast (yellow on dark, white on vibrant)
- **Background:** Blurred or simplified from video frame

**Example Layout:**
```
[Face - Excited] | "I CAN'T" 
  Expression     | "BELIEVE THIS!"
  (Left 40%)     | (Right 60%, stacked)
```

### Formula 2: **Before/After Split**
**CTR Benchmark:** 7-10%

**Elements:**
- **Division:** Vertical line down center or diagonal slash
- **Before:** Left side, less appealing/dramatic
- **After:** Right side, impressive result
- **Labels:** "BEFORE" and "AFTER" in corners
- **Arrow:** Optional, pointing from before to after
- **Colors:** Different color grades for each side

**Example Layout:**
```
BEFORE          |  AFTER
[Darker, plain] | [Bright, vibrant]
                ‚Üí
```

### Formula 3: **Big Numbers + Icon**
**CTR Benchmark:** 6-9%

**Elements:**
- **Number:** Huge, centered or upper third (e.g., "$247" or "24 HOURS")
- **Icon/Emoji:** Related to topic (‚úàÔ∏è üçï üí∞)
- **Supporting Text:** Small, explains context
- **Background:** Relevant image, slightly darkened
- **Color:** Number in contrasting color (often yellow, red, green)

**Example Layout:**
```
       $247 ‚úàÔ∏è
"FLIGHT TO PARIS"
  (background: plane/Eiffel Tower)
```

### Formula 4: **Question Hook**
**CTR Benchmark:** 7-11%

**Elements:**
- **Question:** Direct, relatable (e.g., "IS THIS LEGAL?")
- **Visual:** Shows mysterious or intriguing scene
- **Face:** Optional, confused or shocked expression
- **Colors:** Bright text on dark overlay
- **Punctuation:** Question mark emphasized

**Example Layout:**
```
[Intriguing image with overlay]
        "IS THIS
         LEGAL?"
(Face in corner with confused look)
```

### Formula 5: **Versus Comparison**
**CTR Benchmark:** 8-10%

**Elements:**
- **Two Items:** Side by side
- **VS Symbol:** Center, bold
- **Labels:** Clear identification of each side
- **Visual Difference:** Obvious contrast
- **Colors:** Each side gets distinct color treatment

**Example Layout:**
```

 $50           $500
FLIGHT        FLIGHT
```

### Formula 6: **Arrow Pointing**
**CTR Benchmark:** 6-8%

**Elements:**
- **Arrow:** Large, bright (often red or yellow)

## Video Pacing Guides

### Format 1: **60-Second Travel Reel**
**Goal:** Maximum retention, viral potential

**Pacing Structure:**
- **0-3s:** HOOK - Best moment/stunning visual + text overlay
- **3-10s:** Intro/context - Where/what is this?
- **10-40s:** Main content - 3-5 distinct moments (6-10s each)
- **40-55s:** Climax - Most impressive shot/payoff
- **55-60s:** CTA/closer - "Follow for more" + logo

**Cut Rhythm:**
- Cuts every 2-4 seconds
- Match cuts to music beat (every 2nd or 4th beat)
- Speed ramps at seconds: 5, 15, 25, 35 (keeps energy)

**Music Pacing:**
- Choose track with clear build/drop
- Drop at 40-50s mark for climax
- Fast tempo (120-140 BPM)

**Retention Tricks:**
- Pattern interrupt at 15s and 35s (glitch, freeze, text)
- Camera movement in every clip (no static shots)
- Text reveal every 10-12 seconds

---

### Format 2: **3-5 Minute Vlog**
**Goal:** Story-driven, watch time

**Pacing Structure:**
- **0-5s:** Hook - Tease best moment or ask question
- **5-20s:** Intro - Who/where/what today
- **20s-80%:** Story arcs - 2-4 mini-stories with rises/falls
- **Last 20%:** Resolution/payoff
- **Final 10s:** CTA + endscreen

**Cut Rhythm:**
- Opening: Cuts every 2-3 seconds
- Story sections: Cuts every 4-7 seconds (slower, breathing room)
- Climax: Back to 2-3 second cuts
- Emotional moments: Hold shots 8-12 seconds

**Music Pacing:**
- Background music 20-30% volume
- Music changes between story arcs
- Silence/ambient sound for key moments

**Retention Tricks:**
- Chapter preview at start ("First we do X, then Y, and finally...")
- B-roll every 15-20 seconds minimum
- On-screen graphics/text every 30 seconds
- Pattern change every 45-60 seconds (location, music, topic)

---

### Format 3: **15-30 Second Ad-Style**
**Goal:** Sell feeling/destination in seconds

**Pacing Structure:**
- **0-2s:** Attention grab - Best visual, no setup
- **2-10s:** Build - Quick montage of 4-6 clips
- **10-12s:** Payoff/reveal - The "wow" moment
- **12-15s:** Logo/CTA

**Cut Rhythm:**
- Cuts every 1.5-2 seconds (fast!)
- All clips in rhythm with music
- No clip longer than 3 seconds

**Music Pacing:**
- Dramatic build (orchestral or electronic)
- Drop at 10-second mark for payoff
- High energy throughout

**Retention Tricks:**
- Zero talking - music and visuals only
- Every shot is an "A-roll" (no filler)
- Text overlay: location name or one-word descriptors
- End with "Visit [Destination]" CTA

### Format 4: **10-Minute Tutorial/Guide**
**Goal:** Value delivery, subscriber conversion

**Pacing Structure:**
- **0-30s:** Hook + what you'll learn
- **30s-2min:** Context/why it matters
- **2-8min:** Main content - 3-5 clear sections with breaks
- **8-9min:** Bonus tip/summary
- **9-10min:** CTA, like/subscribe, next video

**Cut Rhythm:**

- B-roll overlay every 20-30 seconds

**Music Pacing:**
- Soft background music throughout
- Fade out during important explanations

**Retention Tricks:**
- Show end result at start

- Variation: Close-up ‚Üí medium ‚Üí wide angles

**Goal:** Emotional impact, artistry

**Pacing Structure:**

- **40s-70%:** Rising action - challenges, journey

**Cut Rhythm:**

**Music Pacing:**

## Mobile Editing Workflows

### Workflow 1: **CapCut Express Edit (Phone)**
**Time: 15-30 minutes for 60s reel**

**Step 1: Import & Organize (3 min)**
1. Import all footage to CapCut
2. Create folders: "A-roll" "B-roll" "Transitions"
3. Watch all clips, mark favorites (star icon)
4. Delete obvious bad takes

**Step 2: Rough Cut (8 min)**
1. Drag starred clips to timeline in story order
2. Trim each clip to 3-7 seconds (cut out dead space)
3. Watch through - does it make sense?
4. Move clips around for better flow

**Step 3: Music & Timing (5 min)**
1. Add music from CapCut library or imported audio
2. Trim video to match music length
3. Align major moments to beat drops
4. Adjust clip lengths to sync with rhythm

**Step 4: Effects & Transitions (7 min)**
1. Add transition every 3-5 clips (velocity, blur, glitch)
2. Apply speed ramps to 2-3 clips for energy
3. Add one "wow" effect at climax moment
4. Color grade: Apply preset to all clips (adjust individual if needed)

**Step 5: Text & Final (7 min)**
1. Add captions/subtitles (auto-caption feature)
2. Intro text at 0-3s
3. CTA text at 55-60s
4. Outro logo/watermark
5. Export: 1080p, 30fps, high quality

**Pro Tips:**
- Work in landscape mode for precision
- Use two-finger pinch to zoom timeline
- Double-tap clip to quickly split
- Save project frequently (auto-save can fail)

### Workflow 2: **InShot Story Edit (Phone)**
**Time: 10-20 minutes for Instagram Story series**

**Step 1: Plan Story Arc (2 min)**

**Step 2: Import & Create First Slide (3 min)**

**Step 3: Add Slide Elements (3 min per slide)**

**Step 5: Export Series (2 min)**
- Export each slide individually
- 1080x1920, highest quality
- Save to phone in order (name 01-story, 02-story, etc.)

**Pro Tips:**

### Workflow 3: **VN Editor Cinematic Edit (Phone)**
**Time: 45-90 minutes for 3-5 min video**

**Step 1: Import & Log (10 min)**
1. Import all footage to VN
2. Create timeline with separate video/audio tracks
3. Watch all footage, note timecodes of best moments
4. Organize into folders by scene

**Step 2: Assembly Edit (20 min)**
1. Build rough cut scene by scene
2. Focus on story flow, not perfection
3. Leave room for B-roll (gaps in A-roll)
4. Add placeholder music to feel pacing

**Step 3: B-Roll Overlay (15 min)**
1. Add B-roll clips above main timeline
2. Use PIP or split-screen for variety
3. Trim B-roll precisely (3-6 second clips)
4. Add subtle zooms to static B-roll (keyframes)

**Step 4: Color Grade (15 min)**
1. Create adjustment layer OR grade clip-by-layer
2. Match white balance across all clips first
3. Apply cinematic LUT or manual grade
4. Fine-tune HSL for specific colors (sky, skin, greens)
5. Add film grain for texture

**Step 5: Audio Mix (10 min)**
1. Balance levels: Dialogue -12dB, Music -20dB, SFX -15dB
2. Add crossfades between audio clips
3. Ducking: Lower music when speaking
4. Add ambient sound under B-roll

**Step 6: Effects & Final (15 min)**
1. Smooth transitions (cross dissolve, blur)
2. Text overlays: Lower thirds, location names
3. Subtle vignette or glow on hero shots
4. Export: 4K or 1080p, 24fps, H.264

**Pro Tips:**
- VN supports LUTs - import your own!
- Use keyframe animations for smooth camera moves
- Render preview before final export to check
- Save project to cloud (VN can crash on large projects)
