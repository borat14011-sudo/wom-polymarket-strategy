**Time:** 4 hours  
**Task:** Plan next 30 days
- Choose topics for each day
- Write captions/hooks
- Schedule filming locations

**Time:** 3 hours

- Set up one location
- Record all videos in one session

- Use templates (faster)
- Export in batches
- Queue for scheduling

- Post scheduled content
- Engage with comments (reply to all)

- Check DMs, respond
