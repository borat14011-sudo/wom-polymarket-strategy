## PHASE 1: BREAKING NEWS (T+0 to T+24h)

### Characteristics
- **Information asymmetry** â€” news breaking, not all market participants aware
- **High volatility** â€” wide spreads, fast moves
- **Emotional reactions** â€” panic/greed at extremes

### Trading Rules

| Factor | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **Direction** | Trade WITH the initial momentum (momentum is king) |
| **Entry** | Within first 30 minutes of headline breaking |
| **Position Size** | Small (25-33% of normal) â€” high uncertainty |
| **Stop Loss** | Tight (1-2% max) â€” news can reverse |
| **Target** | 5-15% move or 4-6 hours â€” whichever comes first |

### Entry Signals (GO LONG/SHORT)
- âœ… Headline breaks on primary sources (Reuters, Bloomberg, SEC filings)
- âœ… Volume spikes 300%+ above average
- âœ… Price breaks pre-news levels decisively
- âœ… Options IV explodes (but you're buying shares, not options)

- ğŸ›‘ Counter-narrative emerges from credible source
- ğŸ›‘ Time stop: 6 hours max (overnight risk)
- ğŸ›‘ Target hit (take profit, don't be greedy)

### Risk Management
- **Never hold overnight** on Phase 1 trades

- **Size down** â€” this is speculative by definition
