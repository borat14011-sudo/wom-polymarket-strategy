```
|------|----------|-----------|---------|--------|-------------|---------|-----|-----------------|
```

**Monthly Analysis:**
- Top 5 converting products
- Best performing platforms
- Best performing content types
- Revenue by category (travel/baby/food)
- Commission vs effort analysis

#### 4. Key Metrics to Track

**Product-Level:**
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Conversion rate
- Earnings per click (EPC)
- Average order value (AOV)
- Return rate (if available)

**Content-Level:**
- Reach/impressions
- Engagement rate
- Link click rate
- Save rate (indicator of future conversions)
- Share rate

**Platform-Level:**
- Total clicks by platform
- Revenue by platform
- Time to conversion (when visible)
- Best performing days/times

#### 5. Affiliate Dashboard Checklist
**Daily (5 min):**
- Check for new sales/commissions
- Note any high-performing links

**Weekly (30 min):**
- Update tracking spreadsheet
- Review top performers
- Identify underperformers to optimize/cut

**Monthly (2 hours):**
- Deep dive analytics
- Content planning based on winners
- Test new programs/products
- Update evergreen content links

#### 6. A/B Testing Framework

**Test Variables:**
- Link placement (bio vs story vs post)
- CTA language ("Shop now" vs "Link in bio" vs "DM for link")
- Product positioning (lifestyle vs features vs price)
- Disclosure placement (top vs bottom)
- Visual style (in-use vs flat lay vs comparison)

**Testing Process:**
1. Choose ONE variable
2. Run for 2-4 weeks minimum
3. Track both clicks AND conversions
4. Implement winner, test next variable

- **80%:** Pure value, entertainment, education, storytelling

### Amazon Associates Optimization

#### Understanding Commission Structure (2024)
| Category | Commission Rate | Strategy |
|----------|----------------|----------|
| Luxury Beauty, Amazon Explore | 10% | Promote premium skincare for travel |
| Physical Books, Kitchen | 4.5% | Cookbooks, travel guides |
| Toys, Furniture, Baby Products | 3% | Core niche! |
| Amazon Fresh, Physical Video Games | 1% | Avoid - low rates |

#### Maximum Earnings Tactics

**1. The 24-Hour Window**
- Customer buys ANYTHING within 24 hours = you earn commission
- Strategy: Promote gateway products (strollers), earn on add-on purchases
- "Shopping spree effect": Promote during known shopping times (Sunday nights, paydays)

**2. Amazon OneLink (International)**
- Tool: Genius Link or Amazon OneLink
- Auto-redirects international audience to their local Amazon
- Increases conversion 20-40% vs lost international traffic

**3. Bounty Program**
- Fixed fees for Amazon programs: Prime ($3), Audible ($5), Amazon Music ($3)
- Promote during free trial periods
- "Road trip essentials" â†’ Audible recommendation

**4. Strategic Product Selection**
- **Best performers:** $20-80 price point
- **Avoid:** Very cheap (<$10, low commissions) or very expensive (>$300, decision paralysis)
- **Bundle strategy:** "Starter kit" posts with 5-7 items

**5. Storefront Optimization**
- Create idea lists: "Travel with Baby", "Kitchen Essentials"
- Update seasonally
- Direct traffic to storefront, not individual products (more browse time = more sales)

### Reality Check: Why Most Earn Less

**Common reasons for underperformance:**
1. **Inconsistent posting** (algorithm penalties)
2. **Generic content** (no trust = no conversions)
3. **Wrong audience** (followers who don't buy)
4. **Poor link placement** (people can't find your links)
5. **No email list** (leaving money on the table)
6. **Only using Instagram** (single point of failure)
7. **Promoting too many products** (decision fatigue)
8. **Not tracking** (repeating mistakes)

### Path to $5,000/Month in Affiliate Income

**Starting from 5,000 followers:**

**Month 1-3: Foundation**
- Post 4-5x/week consistently
- Identify top 10 products you genuinely use
- Set up tracking (UTMs, spreadsheet)
- Goal: $200-500/month

**Month 4-6: Expansion**
- Start email list (lead magnet)

**Month 7-9: Optimization**

**Month 10-12: Scale**
- Automate Pinterest + email sequences
