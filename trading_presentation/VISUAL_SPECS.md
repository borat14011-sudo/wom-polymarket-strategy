# Visual Asset Specifications

## Logo Requirements
- Format: PNG with transparency or SVG
- Minimum size: 500x500px
- Color versions: Full color, white, dark

## Iconography Style
- Style: Line icons, 2px stroke
- Color: Navy (#1a365d) or Gold (#d4af37)
- Size: 64x64px for slide use

### Required Icons (per slide reference)
1. Target/Bullseye - Strategy focus
2. Brain/Mind - Behavioral analysis
3. Robot/Gear - Algorithmic trading
4. Shield - Risk management
5. Chart/Graph - Performance
6. Whale - Whale tracking
7. Bot/Robot - Bot detection
8. Clock/Time - Timing optimization
9. Money/Dollar - Returns
10. Warning - Risk disclosures

## Background Images
### Slide 1 (Title)
- Abstract geometric pattern
- Dark blue gradient overlay
- Subtle blockchain/crypto elements

### Content Slides
- Clean white or light gray
- Subtle watermark pattern optional

### Section Dividers
- Solid navy background
- Gold accent line
- Section title centered

## Typography Specifications

### Font Stack
- Headers: Montserrat, Arial, sans-serif
- Body: Inter, Helvetica, sans-serif

### Sizes
- Title Slide Main: 72pt
- Title Slide Sub: 36pt
- Slide Headers: 48pt
- Subheaders: 32pt
- Body Text: 24pt
- Chart Labels: 16pt
- Footnotes: 14pt

### Weights
- Titles: Bold (700)
- Headers: SemiBold (600)
- Body: Regular (400)
- Emphasis: Medium (500)

## Spacing Guidelines
- Slide padding: 60px from edges
- Section spacing: 40px between elements
- Line height: 1.5 for body text
- Chart margins: 20px from text

## Image Export Settings
- Resolution: 300 DPI
- Format: PNG for charts, JPG for photos
- Color space: RGB
- Transparent backgrounds for overlays
