## DAY 2: FILM (45 minutes)

### You Need:
- Your phone
- Natural light (near window or outside)
- **That's it. No fancy gear.**

### Filming Process:
1. **Open your camera app** (not Instagram yet - film in phone camera for better quality)

2. **Stand somewhere with good light**
   - Face the window, not away from it
   - Or go outside (golden hour = 1 hour before sunset)

3. **Film this simple structure** (record 3 takes, pick the best):
   - **0-3 seconds:** Hook (bold statement)
     - "This Madrid restaurant changed everything..."
   - **3-30 seconds:** The story/tip
     - Show the restaurant, explain what made it special
   - **30-45 seconds:** Payoff + CTA
     - "Follow for more family travel tips!"

4. **Record 2 backup videos while you're filming:**
   - Same location, different angle
   - You'll use these later

**Don't edit yet. Just film. Save raw footage.**
