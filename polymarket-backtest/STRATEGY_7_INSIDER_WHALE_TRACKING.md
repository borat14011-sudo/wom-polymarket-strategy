## 11. RISKS & LIMITATIONS

### Execution Risks
1. **Timing Lag:**
   - Polysights flags ‚Üí Your entry = 5-30 min lag
   - Your entry price worse than insider's
   - **Mitigation:** Enter within 5 min of flag

2. **False Positives:**
   - Not every new wallet is an insider
   - Claimed 85% means 15% are WRONG
   - **Mitigation:** Use stop-losses (-15%)

3. **Whale Exit Risk:**
   - Whale dumps position ‚Üí market crashes
   - You may not exit fast enough
   - **Mitigation:** Set alerts, monitor closely

4. **Slippage:**
   - Large position copy = worse price
   - Low liquidity markets = high slippage
   - **Mitigation:** Only copy on liquid markets (>$100K volume)

### Data Risks
1. **Claimed vs Actual Performance:**
   - 85% and 96% win rates are CLAIMED, not independently verified
   - Bloomberg article cites Polysights, but doesn't audit
   - **Mitigation:** VALIDATE via 6-8 week paper trading

2. **Survivorship Bias:**
   - We only see successful traders (Axios, Domer)
   - Failed whale copiers may not be visible
   - **Mitigation:** Track full population, not just winners

3. **Tool Reliability:**
   - Polysights detection may have false negatives (miss insiders)
   - Polymarket Analytics data may lag
   - **Mitigation:** Use multiple tools, cross-verify

### Regulatory Risks
1. **Future Ban:**
   - Congress could ban insider trading on prediction markets
   - CFTC could issue new regulations
   - **Mitigation:** Monitor legislative developments, be ready to exit

2. **Platform Changes:**
   - Polymarket could restrict insider trading
   - KYC requirements could limit participation
   - **Mitigation:** Diversify across platforms (Kalshi, etc.)
