## üí° PRO TIPS

### Content Creation:
- **Batch film:** Record 10 videos in one session
- **Reuse B-roll:** Same footage, different angles/captions
- **Story first, polish later:** Get 10 "good enough" posts live beats 1 perfect post
- **Mobile-first:** Phone camera is fine, focus on hooks/value

### Monetization:
- **Start with affiliates:** Easiest, no product creation
- **Digital products next:** Once you have 500+ engaged followers
- **Sponsorships after 5K:** Brands take you seriously at 5K+
- **Courses at 10K+:** Need established authority

### Growth:
- **Post daily:** Consistency beats perfection
- **Engage 1 hour/day:** Comments, DMs, collaborations
- **Track what works:** Analytics tell you what to double down on
- **Collaborate:** Other creators = free cross-promotion

### Time Management:
- **Monday:** Batch film all content for week
- **Tuesday:** Edit videos
- **Wednesday:** Schedule posts + write captions
- **Thursday:** Engagement day (comments, DMs, outreach)
- **Friday:** Admin (emails, tracking, planning)
- **Weekend:** Rest or extra filming
