**With Segmentation:**
- Personalized content

- Better engagement and retention

## Re-engagement Sequences

### Why Re-engage?

**Stats:**
- 30-50% of subscribers become inactive within 6 months
- Inactive subscribers hurt deliverability (ESPs see low engagement)
- Cost savings: Cleaning inactive subscribers reduces ESP costs
- Better metrics: Higher open rates with engaged list

**Goal of Re-engagement:**
1. Win back inactive subscribers
2. Clean your list (remove permanently disengaged)
3. Understand why they stopped engaging

---

### When to Trigger Re-engagement

**Criteria for "Inactive":**
- No opens in last 60-90 days
- No clicks in last 90-120 days
- Never engaged with last 10 emails

**Timing:**
- Start re-engagement sequence at 60-day mark
- Give them 3-4 attempts over 14-21 days
- Remove if no engagement after sequence
