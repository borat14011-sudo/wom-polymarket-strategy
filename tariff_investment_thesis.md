- Implementation is administrative (no Congressional approval needed)

- **Untapped tariff potential:** $2.8 trillion

**Protection of Domestic Industries:**

3. **Collection Efficiency:** CBP collection systems are more efficient than modeled — revenue exceeds theoretical calculations
