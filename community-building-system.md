## Discord Server Setup Guide

### Server Structure

#### Essential Channels

**Welcome & Info**
- `#welcome` - Auto-greet new members, server tour
- `#rules` - Clear guidelines (pin important messages)
- `#announcements` - Major updates only (muted by default for others)
- `#faq` - Common questions answered
- `#resources` - Links, documents, helpful content

**Community Engagement**
- `#general-chat` - Main hangout space
- `#introductions` - New member intros
- `#wins-and-celebrations` - Share victories
- `#behind-the-scenes` - Exclusive insider content
- `#feedback-and-suggestions` - Community input

**Content & Value**
- `#content-drop` - Share your latest videos/posts
- `#community-creations` - Member-generated content
- `#learning-corner` - Educational discussions
- `#collab-opportunities` - Partnership proposals

**Support & Help**
- `#questions` - Quick help channel
- `#tech-support` - Technical issues
- `#ticket-support` - Private support via bot

**Fun & Engagement**
- `#memes-and-humor` - Lighthearted fun
- `#off-topic` - Non-niche conversations
- `#challenges` - Weekly/monthly challenges
- `#voice-hangouts` - Voice channel discussion

### Role Structure

#### Tier System

**Free Tiers**
- `@Member` - Default role (basic access)
- `@Active Member` - 10+ messages, 7+ days in server
- `@Valued Contributor` - 50+ helpful messages

**Premium Tiers**
- `@Supporter` - $5/month tier (exclusive channels, early access)
- `@VIP` - $15/month tier (1-on-1 time, special perks)
- `@Inner Circle` - $50/month tier (mastermind access, direct line)

**Special Roles**
- `@Moderator` - Community guardians
- `@Brand Ambassador` - Official representatives
- `@Content Creator` - Fellow creators in your niche
- `@OG Member` - Founding members
- `@Birthday Star` - Auto-assigned on birthdays

**Achievement Roles** (via bots)
- `@First Post` - Made their first message
- `@Conversation Starter` - Created 5+ engaged threads
- `@Helper` - Answered 10+ questions
- `@Event Champion` - Attended 3+ events

```markdown
**[Server Name] Community Rules**

## Facebook Group Strategy

### Growth Tactics

#### Setup Optimization

**Group Name Formula:**
- Primary: `[Niche] + Community/Tribe/Network`
- Examples: "E-Commerce Empire Builders", "Fitness Transformation Tribe"

**Description (First 3 Lines Matter Most):**
```
[Solve their biggest pain point] + [Promise transformation]

This is THE community for [target audience] who want to [desired outcome].

Inside: Daily tips, expert Q&As, exclusive trainings, and a supportive tribe cheering you on!
```

**Cover Photo Elements:**
- Your face/brand
- Community name
- Key benefit statement
- Visual of community vibe (happy people, results)

**Group Questions (Screening):**
1. What's your biggest challenge with [topic] right now?
2. How did you hear about this community?
3. I agree to the group rules (Yes/No)
4. What do you hope to get from this group?
5. Your Instagram/website (optional - helps filter spam)

#### Growth Strategies

**1. Announcement Post Method**
- Create engaging content
- In comments: "Want more? Join my free [niche] community at [link]"
- Works on: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn

**2. Collaboration Cross-Promotion**
- Partner with similar-sized creators
- Guest post in each other's groups
- "Collab of the Week" features

**3. Content Upgrades**
- "Download the full guide in our free community"
- Link to group in video descriptions
- Lead magnets that require group membership

**4. Paid Ads (Facebook)**
- Engagement objective (join group)
- Target: Lookalike audiences of your page/email list
- Budget: $5-10/day to start

**5. YouTube End Screen**
- "Join 10,000+ others in our free community"
- Visual CTA in last 20 seconds

**6. Email Signature**
- Add group link to every email
- "P.S. Join our community of [X] members!"

**7. Strategic Tagging**
- Answer questions in other groups (add value first)
- Invite specific people to join your group (don't spam)

## Community Moderation Guide

### Moderation Philosophy

**Core Principles:**
1. **Safety First** - Protect members from harm, harassment, hate
2. **Transparency** - Clear rules, consistent enforcement
3. **Education Over Punishment** - Warn before banning when possible
4. **Context Matters** - Consider intent, history, situation
5. **Community Standards** - Let community values guide decisions

### Moderation Levels

**Level 1: Automated Filters**
- Spam links (repeated URLs)
- Excessive caps (all caps messages)
- Banned words (slurs, explicit content)
- Rapid messaging (flood protection)

**Level 2: Bot Warnings**
- Triggered by keyword filters
- Auto-delete + DM warning
- 3 warnings = temporary mute

**Level 3: Moderator Review**
- Reported content
- Ambiguous violations
- Pattern behavior
- Appeals

**Level 4: Admin Decision**
- Serious violations
- Permanent bans
- Policy changes
- Legal issues

### Rule Violations & Responses

#### Spam

**Definition:**
- Repeated self-promotion
- Excessive posting
- Unsolicited DMs
- Bot behavior

**Response:**
- 1st offense: Warning + post removed
- 2nd offense: 24h mute
- 3rd offense: Permanent ban

**Moderator Script:**
```
Hey [Name], your post was removed for spam. Please review our rules about self-promotion - it's only allowed in [designated channel]. Thanks for understanding!
```

#### Disrespect/Personal Attacks

**Definition:**
- Name-calling
- Insults
- Targeted harassment
- Bullying

**Response:**
- 1st offense: Warning (public or DM depending on severity)
- 2nd offense: 48h-7 day mute
- 3rd offense: Permanent ban

**Moderator Script:**
```
[Name], personal attacks aren't allowed here. You can disagree with ideas without attacking people. Please keep it respectful going forward.
```

#### Misinformation

**Definition:**
- Deliberately false information
- Dangerous advice
- Conspiracy theories
- Scams

**Response:**
- Correct publicly
- Remove post
- Warning
- If repeated: ban

**Moderator Script:**
```
This information isn't accurate. Here's the correct info: [link/explanation]. Please verify information before sharing in the future.
```

#### Off-Topic/Low-Effort Content

**Definition:**
- Wrong channel
- Memes in serious channels
- One-word responses
- Derailing conversations

**Response:**
- Move to correct channel (if possible)
- Gentle redirect
- Remove if disruptive

**Moderator Script:**
```
Hey [Name]! This is great, but it belongs in [#other-channel]. Mind reposting there? Thanks!
```

#### NSFW/Inappropriate Content

**Definition:**
- Sexual content
- Graphic violence
- Disturbing imagery
- Inappropriate jokes

**Response:**
- Immediate removal
- Warning or instant ban (depending on severity)
- Report to platform if illegal

**Moderator Script:**
```
[Name], this content violates our community standards. Please review our rules. [Warning/Ban depending on severity]
```

#### Drama/Conflict

**Definition:**
- Heated arguments
- Bringing external drama
- Call-out posts
- Stirring the pot

**Response:**
- Lock thread if escalating
- Ask participants to DM or drop it
- Mute if they continue
- Address issues privately

**Moderator Script:**
```
This conversation is getting heated. Let's take a breather. If you need to discuss further, please do so in DMs. Thanks everyone.
```

### Handling Difficult Situations

**Scenario 1: Mass Spam Attack**
- Enable slowmode (limits message frequency)
- Temporarily restrict new member permissions
- Ban offending accounts
- Clean up spam messages
- Announce to community what happened

**Scenario 2: Prominent Member Violates Rules**
- No special treatment
- Enforce rules consistently
- Handle privately when possible
- Be prepared to explain decision

**Scenario 3: Moderator Makes Mistake**
- Acknowledge it
- Apologize if needed
- Reverse decision if appropriate
- Learn and document

**Scenario 4: Controversial Topic Emerges**
- Remind everyone of rules
- Keep conversation civil
- Lock if it becomes unproductive
- Consider creating guidelines for that topic

**Scenario 5: False Report**
- Review context
- Message reporter (educate on proper use)
- If repeated false reports: warn or mute

**Scenario 6: Appeal/Complaint About Moderation**
- Review with fresh eyes
- Get second opinion
- Explain reasoning
- Reverse if error, stand firm if correct

### Ban Process

**When to Ban Immediately:**
- Illegal content
- Doxxing/sharing personal info
- Severe harassment
- Raid/coordinated attack
- Spam bots

**When to Warn First:**
- First-time minor violations
- Misunderstandings
- Accidental rule breaks
- Borderline cases

**How to Ban:**
1. Document the violation (screenshots)
2. Consult with another mod if time permits
3. Execute ban
4. Log it (who, what, when, why)
5. DM the person explaining why (optional but recommended)

**Ban Message Template:**

This decision was made because [explanation].

Our community rules are here: [link]
```

**Community Building:**
- Start conversations
