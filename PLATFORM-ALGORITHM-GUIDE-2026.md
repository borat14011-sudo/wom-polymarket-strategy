## üéØ UNIVERSAL ALGORITHM PRINCIPLES (All Platforms)

### Rule 1: Watch Time is King
**What it means:** Platforms want people on their app longer  
**Your job:** Make content that keeps people watching

**How to measure:**
- Instagram: Average watch time (in Insights)
- TikTok: Watch time % (aim for 60%+ completion rate)
- YouTube: Average view duration (aim for 50%+)

**How to improve:**
- Hook in first 1-3 seconds
- Payoff at the very end (keep them watching)
- No fluff in the middle

**What it means:** 1,000 engaged followers > 100,000 ghost followers

**Engagement rate calculation:**
```
(Likes + Comments + Saves + Shares) √∑ Followers √ó 100 = Engagement Rate %
```

**Benchmarks:**
- üî• Excellent: 8-12%

**How to improve:**
- Ask questions in captions
- Reply to every comment (algorithm reward)
- Create "save-worthy" content (checklists, guides)

---

### Rule 3: Consistency is Weighted
**What it means:** Algorithm remembers if you post regularly

**Posting patterns the algorithm loves:**
- Daily posting for 30+ days
- Same time of day (predictable)
- No gaps longer than 3 days

**Posting patterns that hurt you:**
- Post 5x one week, 0x next week
- Random times (11 AM Monday, 9 PM Thursday)
- Long breaks (2+ weeks off)

**What it means:** Posting native content > posting links to other platforms

**Examples:**

## üì± INSTAGRAM ALGORITHM 2026

### How It Works (Simplified):

**Step 1:** Instagram shows your post to 10-20% of followers  
**Step 2:** If engagement is good ‚Üí Show to more followers + Explore page  
**Step 3:** If engagement is excellent ‚Üí Push to non-followers via Reels tab  

### What Instagram Values (In Order):

1. **Saves** (highest weight)
   - Why: Indicates valuable content worth revisiting
   - How to get: Educational, reference content, checklists

2. **Shares** (second highest)
   - Why: Shows content is share-worthy
   - How to get: Relatable, funny, or controversial content

3. **Comments** (third)
   - Why: Shows content sparked conversation
   - How to get: Ask questions, controversial opinions

4. **Likes** (lowest weight, but still matters)
   - Why: Basic engagement signal
   - How to get: Entertaining content

5. **Watch Time** (for Reels)
   - Why: Keeps users on platform
   - How to get: Hook + loop (watch again for details)

### Instagram Reels Specific Tips:

**‚úÖ DO:**
- Use trending audio (Instagram suggests these)
- Add text captions (85% watch without sound)
- Keep it 7-15 seconds (sweet spot for rewatches)
- Post during peak hours (check your Insights)
- Use 3-5 hashtags (not 30!)

**‚ùå DON'T:**
- Repost TikToks with watermark (instant suppression)
- Use copyrighted music without license
- Post blurry/low-quality video
- Ask for likes/follows in caption (spammy)

### Instagram Stories Algorithm:

**How it ranks your story in followers' feeds:**
1. How often they interact with your content
2. How recently they viewed your stories
3. How much they've DM'd you

**Strategy:**
- Use engagement stickers (polls, questions, sliders)
- Reply to everyone who DMs you
- Post 5-10 stories/day (consistent visibility)

### Hashtag Strategy 2026:

**Old way (2022):** 30 hashtags, mix of big and small  
**New way (2026):** 3-5 highly relevant hashtags

**Why the change:** Instagram prioritizes content quality over hashtag stuffing

**How to choose:**
1. 1-2 broad hashtags (#Travel #FamilyTravel) - 500K-2M posts
2. 1-2 niche hashtags (#MadridWithBaby #ToddlerTravel) - 10K-100K posts
3. 1 branded hashtag (yours, like #BoratTravels)

## üéµ TIKTOK ALGORITHM 2026

### How It Works:

**For You Page (FYP) is the game.** Getting on FYP = viral potential.

**TikTok's Algorithm Decision Flow:**
1. Your video is shown to 200-500 people (test batch)
2. If engagement is above threshold ‚Üí Shown to 1,000-5,000 people
3. If still strong ‚Üí Shown to 10,000-50,000 people
4. If excellent ‚Üí Viral (100K-1M+ views)

**At each stage, algorithm measures:**
- Watch time % (most important)
- Likes, comments, shares
- Profile visits, follows
- Rewatches

### What TikTok Values:

**1. Completion Rate (CRITICAL)**
- Goal: 60%+ of viewers watch to the end
- How: Short videos (7-15 sec) OR loop format (rewatch to catch detail)

**2. Rewatch Rate**
- Goal: People watch 2-3 times
- How: Hide detail in background, fast-paced editing, "wait for it" moments

**3. Engagement in First Hour**
- Goal: High like/comment ratio in first 60 min
- How: Post when your audience is active, engage with comments immediately

**4. Trending Sounds**
- TikTok LOVES when you use trending sounds
- How: Check "Discover" page, look for sounds with üìà arrow

### TikTok Specific Tactics:

**Hook Formula (First 1 Second):**
- Pattern interrupt (unexpected visual/sound)
- Bold text on screen
- Controversial statement

**Examples:**
- ‚ùå "Hey guys, in today's video..."
- ‚úÖ [Shocking image] "This ruined our Madrid trip"

**Loop Technique:**
- Video ends where it begins (seamless)
- Or: "Watch again to spot the [thing]"
- Result: 2-3x watch time

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- 3-5 hashtags
- Always include #FYP or #ForYou (yes, it actually helps)
- 1-2 niche tags (#BabyTravel)
- 1-2 broad tags (#Travel)

### TikTok Algorithm Hacks:

**1. Delete and Repost (Within 1 Hour)**
- If video gets <50 views in first hour, delete and repost
- Different time, different caption
- Sometimes algorithm misjudges initial batch

**2. Pin Your Best Video**
- Your top performer should be pinned to profile
- New visitors see it first = more follows

**3. Duet/Stitch Trending Videos**
- Algorithm boosts duets/stitches
- Ride the wave of someone else's viral video

**4. Post 1-3x Per Day**
- TikTok rewards frequent posting more than Instagram
- Sweet spot: 2x per day (morning + evening)

## üé¨ YOUTUBE SHORTS ALGORITHM 2026

### How It Works:

YouTube Shorts competes with TikTok/Reels, so algorithm is AGGRESSIVE in pushing good content.

**Key Difference from TikTok:** 
- TikTok = Pure watch time
- YouTube = Click-through rate (CTR) + watch time

### What YouTube Shorts Values:

**1. CTR (Click-Through Rate) - NEW FOCUS**
- Your thumbnail appears in Shorts feed
- If people swipe past = bad signal
- If people tap to watch = good signal
- Goal: 10%+ CTR

**How to improve:**
- Eye-catching thumbnail (yes, even on Shorts!)
- Bright colors, faces, text overlay
- Curiosity gap ("You won't believe...")

**2. Watch Time %**
- Same as TikTok: aim for 60%+
- YouTube tracks "swipes" (did they swipe away?)

**3. Engagement (Likes, Comments, Subscribes)**
- YouTube REALLY cares about subscribes
- Ask for subscribe in video ("Subscribe for more travel tips!")

**4. Session Time**
- Did viewer watch your Short, then watch more Shorts?
- Or did they leave YouTube?
- Goal: Keep them on YouTube

### YouTube Shorts Specific Tips:

**Title Optimization:**
- Unlike TikTok, YouTube Shorts title matters
- Include keyword (for search)
- Example: "Madrid Baby Travel Mistakes to Avoid"

**Description Matters:**
- First 2 lines show in feed
- Include main keyword
- Add timestamps if applicable

**Hashtags:**
- #Shorts (REQUIRED - tells algorithm it's a Short)
- 2-3 niche hashtags (#BabyTravel #MadridTravel)

**End Screen CTA:**
- "Subscribe for Part 2!"
- Point to another video
- YouTube loves when viewers watch multiple videos

## üìå PINTEREST ALGORITHM 2026

### How It Works:

Pinterest is a **search engine**, not social media. Algorithm is based on SEO, not engagement.

**Pin Lifecycle:**
- Day 1-7: Pinterest tests your pin (small audience)
- Day 8-30: If performing well, pushes to broader audience
- Month 2-6: Evergreen pins continue getting views
- Month 6+: Older pins can resurface if still relevant

### What Pinterest Values:

**1. Pin Quality Score**
- Original content > repins
- Vertical format (2:3 ratio, 1000x1500px)
- Clear, readable text overlay
- High-res image

**2. Engagement Signals**
- Saves (most important)
- Click-throughs to your site
- Close-ups (zooming in on pin)

**3. Keyword Optimization**
- Pin title must include keyword
- Pin description (500 characters max)
- Board name
- Image ALT text

**4. Consistency**
- 5-10 pins per day (use scheduler)
- Mix of fresh pins + repins

### Pinterest Tactics:

**Keyword Research:**
- Search bar suggestions (Pinterest tells you what's popular)
- Trends tool (trends.pinterest.com)
- Competitor boards (what keywords do they use?)

**Pin Design Formula:**
- Top third: Bold headline with keyword
- Middle: Eye-catching image
- Bottom third: CTA + your brand/logo

**Board Strategy:**
- 10-20 boards (topic-specific)
- Board titles = keywords ("Baby Travel Tips" not "Cute Babies")
- 30-50 pins per board minimum

**Seasonal Content:**
- Pin 45-60 days BEFORE season (Christmas pins in November)
- Pinterest users plan ahead

## üê¶ TWITTER/X ALGORITHM 2026

### How It Works:

"For You" tab uses algorithm. "Following" tab is chronological (no algorithm).

**Algorithm weighs:**
1. Recency (new tweets prioritized)
2. Engagement (likes, retweets, replies)
3. Your past interactions (accounts you engage with often)
4. Tweet type (media tweets > text-only)

### What Twitter Values:

**1. Engagement in First 15 Minutes**
- Critical window
- If tweet gets 10+ likes in 15 min ‚Üí Algorithm boosts
- If 0 likes ‚Üí Dies quickly

**2. Media (Images/Videos)**
- Tweets with media get 2-3x more engagement
- Video > GIF > Image > Text-only

**3. Thread Engagement**
- Threads (1/10, 2/10, etc.) get higher reach
- Hook in first tweet, value in thread

**4. Replies & Conversations**
- Tweets that spark replies = higher reach
- Ask questions, controversial takes

### Twitter Tactics:

**Best Times to Post:**
- Weekdays: 9-11 AM, 1-3 PM, 7-9 PM (local time)
- Avoid: Late night, very early morning

**Tweet Formula:**
- Hook (first line)
- Value (middle)
- CTA (last line)

**Example:**
```
I spent $0 on our Madrid trip with a 7-month-old.

Here's how: [thread with 8 tips]

Follow @YourHandle for more family travel hacks.
```

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- 1-2 hashtags max (unlike Instagram)
- Niche tags (#FamilyTravel) perform better than broad (#Travel)

## üíº LINKEDIN ALGORITHM 2026

### How It Works:

LinkedIn wants **dwell time** (time spent reading your post).

**Algorithm flow:**
1. Shows post to small % of connections
2. If engagement good (especially comments) ‚Üí Broader network
3. If excellent ‚Üí "Trending" status

### What LinkedIn Values:

**1. Dwell Time**
- Longer posts = more reading time = good signal
- Sweet spot: 1,200-1,500 characters

**2. Comments > Likes**
- LinkedIn prioritizes conversation
- Reply to every comment (boosts post)

**3. Native Content**
- Text posts > link posts
- LinkedIn wants to keep you on LinkedIn

**4. Early Engagement**
- First hour is critical
- Tag 3-5 people in comments to kickstart engagement

### LinkedIn Tactics:

**Post Format (Hook + Story + Lesson):**
```
[Hook - Bold Statement]

I quit my job to become a full-time travel creator.

[Story - 3-5 paragraphs]

Here's what happened...
[Tell story with ups/downs]

[Lesson]

What I learned: [Key takeaway]

[CTA]

What would you do in my situation?
```

**Hashtags:**
- 3-5 relevant tags
- Mix popular + niche

**Timing:**
- Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM
- Avoid weekends (lower engagement)

## üö® ALGORITHM CHANGES & HOW TO ADAPT

### When Algorithms Change (And They Will):

**Signs of Algorithm Shift:**
- Sudden drop in reach (50%+ down)
- Other creators reporting same issue
- Platform announces update

**How to Adapt:**

**1. Monitor Creator Discussions**
- Twitter search: "[Platform] algorithm"
- Facebook groups for creators
- Reddit r/Instagram, r/TikTok, etc.

**2. Test New Formats**
- If Reels drop, try Stories
- If text posts drop, try video
- Experiment for 7-14 days

**3. Analyze What's Working NOW**
- Look at viral posts from last 7 days
- What format? What hook? What topic?
- Replicate structure (not content)

**4. Don't Panic**
- Algorithm changes affect everyone
- Temporary dips are normal
- Keep posting through the dip
