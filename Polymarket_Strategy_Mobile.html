Market Reality: The 4% Fee Barrier

  • Round‑Trip Fee: 2% entry + 2% exit = 4% total.
  • Slippage: 0.5‑3% depending on liquidity.
  • Gas Fees: ~$0.50 per round‑trip (fixed).
  • Implication: Strategies need >4% gross edge to be profitable.

Favorites vs Longshots: Backtesting shows favorites (>80%) have negative expected value after fees. Longshots (<20%) offer positive edge due to higher payout multipliers.
